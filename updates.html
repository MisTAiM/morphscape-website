Updates

What's changed recently.

Best-in-slot gear pass

Added Scythe of Vitur, Tumeken's Shadow, Zaryte Crossbow, Avernic Defender, full Torva, full Masori, Amulet of Torture, Necklace of Anguish, Tormented Bracelet, and the (i) ring variants. Corrected Sanguinesti Staff's magic attack bonus and level requirement, which were swapped.

Crash fix on new gear

Equipping any of the new best-in-slot items was crashing the client outright - a newer model format used by those items wasn't being decoded. Fixed.

Visual fixes

Fixed Torva full helm and Masori mask clipping through hair. Fixed Torva platebody and Masori body clipping through arms. Fixed Scythe of Vitur's walk/turn/run animations, which were playing the wrong frames.

Known issue: Torva platelegs and Masori chaps still clip at the waist - not fixed yet.

Store & website

Added an in-game donation store - purchases are claimed with ::redeem. Launched this website with a working Store page. Checkout itself isn't live yet; see the Store page for details.
