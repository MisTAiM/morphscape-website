Support Morphscape

Morphscape is free to play, always. If the world we're building means something to you, you can help fund the work behind it.

Donations never affect gameplay. They don't grant items, coins, ranks, or any in-game advantage - and they never will. Morphscape stays 100% free to play for everyone. What your support funds is the original work: the Morphscape Saga story, the world and its art, the music, and the countless hours of development. The rewards below are original digital content and community perks, delivered outside the game.

Think of this like backing an indie creator, not buying power. Every tier is a thank-you made of things we actually made and own - lore, art, music, and a place in the community - never a shortcut through the game.
