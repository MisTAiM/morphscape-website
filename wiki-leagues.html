Leagues

A full League game mode built around 7 relic tiers, a standalone task list, and the Divine Pact god-alignment tree - inspired by August RSPS's Pantheon League.

7 relic tiers 32 relics to choose from 74 League tasks 6 Divine Pact gods 15 pact nodes per god

How it works

Sign up for League mode through the Gielinor Guide. Every League task you complete pays League Points and a small amount of Divine Pact points. Cross a tier's point threshold and you automatically get that tier's passive XP and drop-rate bonus, plus a one-time prompt to choose an active relic from that tier - a choice that's locked in permanently once made. Passive bonuses stack across every tier you've cleared; active relics are one pick per tier, so across a full run you'll end up with 7 chosen relics total.

League Tasks

74 tasks spread across three types - reach a skill level, rack up a cumulative kill count against a monster (or list of monster variants), or receive a specific item as a monster drop for the first time. Every task pays League Points on completion (6,415 points on offer across the full list) plus a small amount of Divine Pact points, worth a minimum of 1 and roughly 1 for every 20 League Points the task pays. Talk to the League Guide or use ::leaguetasks to see your point total and the next 15 incomplete tasks.

The Divine Pact

A second, separate points economy that runs alongside the relic tiers. Align to one of 6 gods at the Pact Keeper (a permanent choice, short of a full reset), then spend Divine Pact points on that god's 15-node tree - no fixed order, buy whatever you can afford. Each god's tree caps out at an "Avatar" node combining two of that god's effects. You get up to 8 full tree resets per character, which refund every point you've spent.

Relics and pacts stack independently. A relic's XP/drop-rate bonus and a pact node's XP/drop-rate bonus both apply on top of each other - there's no overlap penalty for investing in both systems.
